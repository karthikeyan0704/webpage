G-SHOCK Full Metal

Combining Toughness of Spirit with the Wonders of Technology

The very first G-SHOCK made a powerful impression, staking a global claim as the brand of absolute toughness. With a form born of a single-minded commitment to shock resistance, it endures yet today as an eternal standard. That same heritage runs throughG-SHOCK Full Metal design, which combines toughness of spirit with the wonders of technology. Original Casio manufacturing processes and cutting-edge technologies are applied to stainless steel and titanium materials, ensuring shock resistance in simple, elegant designs boasting both strength and beauty. Truth to origin and constant innovation. All for those who appreciate totally unique toughness-oriented watch design. The G-SHOCK Full Metal line stands as a testament to the proud G-SHOCK quest for advancement and untiring innovation.

The GMW-B5000 debuted in 2018, achieving the design of the very first G-SHOCK in full metal form to usher in a new era of G-SHOCK development. The G-SHOCK Full Metal line features designs based on the iconic 5000 and 2100 styles. Heir to the spirit of the very firstG-SHOCK, the lineup incorporates new shock-resistant structures with both toughness and practicality. Each individual timepiece is painstakingly assembled and finished by hand, reflecting the committed spirit of precision watch production unique to Yamagata Casio, the “mother factory” ofG-SHOCK.

Ever since the establishment of the brand,G-SHOCK design has been defined by a passion for construction, functionality, and materials, along with a commitment to taking toughness to the limits, and beyond. Innovative technologies and materials have been incorporated into manufacturing processes, as well, guided by revolutionary concepts not seen anywhere else in the timepiece industry.

The original shock-resistant structure, born from a vision of constructing a watch that wouldn’t break even if dropped, is a true example of this. For the G-SHOCK Full Metal line, sophisticated technical approaches and discerning design sensibilities fostered over many years are integrated to develop a new, original shock-resistant construction optimized for metal materials, challenging the limits of shock-resistant performance.
